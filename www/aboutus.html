 About ChronosLog and its milestones

 

ChronosLog is a cloud-first software company that helps teams at 12,000+ companies—SMBs and large-scale enterprises—collaborate, plan and schedule resources, manage budgets, and track time directly from their daily workflow. ChronosLog started as a homegrown time tracking solution, which offered our software and business teams seamless integration with Jira. It changed the way we worked. 

We quickly realized this technology could help every team and business gain visibility over their work efforts for better collaboration and decision-making. Today, ChronosLog is a team of 100 peers with offices in Reykjavík, Iceland and Montréal, Canada. 

We are one of the largest, award-winning, top-selling developers in the Atlassian ecosystem. ChronosLog offers three efficiency and visibility-enhancing products that extend Jira to help IT, software development, consultancy, and business teams work smarter.
